Loan Programs

Find the Right Loan for You

We offer a wide range of mortgage products to fit your financial goals, whether you're buying your first home or investing in property.

Conventional Loans

Traditional financing with competitive rates for borrowers with strong credit. Available in fixed-rate and adjustable-rate options with flexible terms.

  • Down payments as low as 3%
  • 15, 20, and 30-year terms
  • Primary, second home & investment

FHA Loans

Government-backed loans designed for first-time homebuyers and those with lower credit scores. More flexible qualification requirements make homeownership accessible.

  • Down payments as low as 3.5%
  • Credit scores from 580+
  • Gift funds allowed for down payment

VA Loans

Exclusive financing for eligible veterans, active-duty military, and surviving spouses. One of the best mortgage benefits available for those who served.

  • No down payment required
  • No private mortgage insurance
  • Competitive interest rates

Jumbo Loans

Financing for high-value properties that exceed conforming loan limits. Ideal for luxury homes and high-cost areas in California.

  • Loan amounts above $766,550
  • Fixed and adjustable rates
  • Flexible down payment options

DSCR / Investor Loans

Designed for real estate investors, these loans qualify based on the property's rental income rather than personal income. Perfect for building a rental portfolio.

  • No personal income verification
  • Based on property cash flow
  • Close in LLC or entity name

Refinance Loans

Lower your monthly payment, shorten your loan term, or tap into your home's equity. Multiple refinance options to help you optimize your mortgage.

  • Rate & term refinance
  • Cash-out refinance
  • FHA/VA streamline options
